Fruit Preserves

First, pick Song, Artist or Genre from the drop down menu.
Then type in one of your favorites and let the suggested jams be picked for you! If you dont like the suggestions or want to try something different, just search again!
Once your suggested jams have appeared, listen to the sample to see if you like it enough to add it to your playlist!
If you think that jam is worth preserving, click the save-to-playlist button and it will be saved in your playlist while you search for more jams!
Finally, if you're really jamming away and want to see if your new favorite artist has any concerts coming up, click on the check-out-upcoming-concerts button.
It will bring up any upcoming shows with a link to buy tickets!
